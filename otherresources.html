An Hour With God

21 Day Challenge

Spend an hour with God for 21 days straight.

By the end of the 21 day challenge, spending an hour with God should be a habit. Make sure that it isn't just a habit, but it is from the heart and you are spending an hour with God because you love Him and want to talk with Him as you would a friend.

By the end of the challenge it should be pretty easy to spend an hour with God in prayer than it was 21 days earlier.
