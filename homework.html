Praising the Lord

Answers

Homework

1. Read Psalms 145. While reading the verses write down the things you see that God is and what He has done and what He does. Basically look for the things in the verses that you can praise God for.

2. After writing down the things you can praise God for, in your praying time, use those things in your prayer when you are praising the Lord. You will repeat this process until you have finished the chapter, then we will check your work in the next lesson. NOTE: you will not get graded for this! Just do your best.

Praising the Lord with singing

Verses Of Song

Homework

1. For the first day read and think about what it says in the first verse in Holy Holy Holy then pray through it. Repeat this process until you finish all the verses of the song.

2. Then for the next 2 days sing the first 2 verses of the hymn, then the next two the next day.

3. On the last day read, think, pray, and sing through all the verses.
