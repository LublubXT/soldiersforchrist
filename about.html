About the Team

My purpose of starting this team, is to turn teenagers that want to follow God, or don't know how to follow God, into Soldiers for Christ. I have a burden for teens and I feel that we teens should be doing more than school, TV, and video games all day. God has a purpose for all of us and a command for all that are His children, and that call (command) is "The Great Commision". God told all of His followers to go unto all the world preaching the Gospel. Many people have change the Great Commission from a command to a sudgestion, but it is no sudgestion. God doesn't sudgest things He commands things.

About Me

My name is Caleb Gross. I am a missionary kid to a small country in western europe called Moldova, and I love it here. I am 15 almost 16 and I love my Lord. He has done so much for me, and He has given me so much. I owe Him my very life, and I hope to serve Him in the Ministry for as long as I am able. I love studying Gods word (kjv), and teaching from it. I have a burden for the lost, especially teens. I have started this team because I wan't other teens to experience some of the things I do, like walking with God and seeing Him work is their own lives and in the lives of others.
