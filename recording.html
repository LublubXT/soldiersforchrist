Lesson 16

Examination & Planification

Plan your battle to win

Knowing how to fight your spiritual battles is very important. In this lesson, I explain how to make your battle plan, and how to use it. In these next lessons, we will be discussing how to prepare yourself to fight and how you should fight. You can check out the outline of "Plan Your Battle to Win". I wrote this back in March when I was in Romania.

Three questions to ask yourself:
1. What is the root of my sin?
2. Why do I do this sin?
3. Why do I have trouble having victory over this sin?

Home Work!
Examine yourself and make your battle plan for your sins.

Lesson 15

Understanding who we are and who God is.

The only reason why we are something is because of Jesus Christ. We are but dust as it says in Psalms. We came from the dust of the earth. God on the other-hand is everything that is good. He is righteous, unlike us. He is Holy, unlike us. He is Mighty, unlike us.

Get your perspective on God and not on yourself. If your perspective is on yourself, you are setting yourself up for destruction and lots of regrets. On the other hand, if our perspective is on God, we will succeed and have joy.


Lesson 14

Plan Your Battle to Win - Purification

If we want to have victory over our spiritual battles, then we must make sure that we don't have any unconfessed sins in our lives.

Step One: Make sure you don't have any unconfessed sins, and if so, confess them. God is faithful and just to forgive us our sins.
