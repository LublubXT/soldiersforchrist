Soldiers for Christ

"Thou therefore endure hardness, as a good soldier of Jesus Christ. No man that warreth entangleth himself with the affairs of this life; that he may please him who hath chosen him to be a soldier. And if a man also strive for masteries, yet is he not crowned, except he strive lawfully."

II Timothy 2:3-5

Purpose

My purpose of starting this team, is to turn teenagers that want to follow God, or don't know how to follow God, into Soldiers for Christ. I have a burden for teens and I feel that we teens should be doing more than school, TV, and video games all day. God has a purpose for all of us and a command for all that are His children, and that call (command) is "The Great Commision". God told all of His followers to go unto all the world preaching the Gospel. Many people have change the Great Commission from a command to a sudgestion, but it is no sudgestion. God doesn't sudgest things He commands things.

What do we talk about?

The topics that we discuss is walking with God, fighting your spiritual battles, and making disciples for the Lord.

Who is this for?

If you are within the age of 12 and 20, then we want you! There aren't really qualifications to join, but the purpose of this team is to turn an average Christian teen into a Soldier of Christ. This team is focused on those that are Christians not for the lost.

What do we do?

About every week on Saturday we have a meeting that is hosted on Google Meet. In the meetings we have a Bible Lesson or Discussion, Prayer time, and a chat time. Meetings can last from 45 minutes to several hours, but that is rare.

Why should you join?

We (Christians) should all strive to have a close and personal walk God. You might be wondering "Why should I have a close walk with God? What's wrong with how it is now?" let me ask you something, how much time do you spend in prayer and reading God's word? Five minutes maybe ten? Let me tell you that is not even close to enough. In this course you will learn how to walk with God and pray for over an hour if not longer. You also might be wondering "Why do I need to know how to fight my spiritual battles?" Let me ask you another question to answer your question. How do you fight your spiritual battles? Are you prepared with the word of God? Do you have on the whole armor of God? Who are you depending on to fight? Yourself or God?

Want to join?

If you want to grow your walk with the Lord, please do consider joining my team. I would love to have you. Just contact me. If you have any questions please feel free to ask.
